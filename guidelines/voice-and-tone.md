# Voice and Tone

How Envision speaks. Sourced from the Envision Brand Book (2026 Edition), Sections 6 and 7.

---

## The Voice Philosophy

Envision's voice is a **knowledgeable guide — not a cheerleader.**

We are experts who uplift, guide, and empower people to thrive. Clear, human, and concise. Confident but warm, always focused on outcomes.

Our voice is not a single setting. It shifts depending on who we're speaking to and which part of Envision is doing the speaking. What never changes is our commitment to language that includes, uplifts, and centers the people we serve.

## Our Voice Is

- **Confident** — We speak with clarity and credibility. We know our work and back it up with real stories and real results.
- **Purposeful** — Everything we say ties back to our mission. We're not just good, we're effective.
- **Grounded** — We meet people where they are. No jargon. Genuine conversations.

## Our Voice Is Not

- Gloomy, divisive, or arrogant.
- Jargon-heavy or self-centered.
- Paternalistic — we do not "make people thrive." People thrive. We enable it.
- Vague — claims should be measurable where possible.

## Tone by Pillar

Voice shifts depending on which pillar is speaking and who the audience is.

| Pillar | Tone |
| --- | --- |
| Envision Arts Center | Expressive, celebratory, community-rooted. Lead with the work, not the diagnosis. |
| Envision Base Supply Center | Direct, earned, no-nonsense. Operational reliability and shared values. Never performative. |
| Envision Child Development Center | Warm, parent-facing, community-driven. Future-ready and outcome-grounded. |
| Envision Commercial Solutions | Business-first, capability-led, credibility-forward. Mission is background unless context requires it. |
| Envision Mission Services | Compassionate, dignified, community-centered. Never pitying. |
| Envision Research Institute | Precise, credible, forward-looking. Specificity is credibility. |
| Envision Vision Rehabilitation Center | Warm with clinical credibility. Hope-forward, grounded in real outcomes. |
| Envision Workforce Innovation | Motivating, practical, outcome-focused. Coaching not cheerleading. |
| Continuing Education | Authoritative and collegial. Write to the credential level of the audience. |

Full pillar guidance: `pillar-profiles.md`.

## Good Language Examples

- "Tools to thrive at work"
- "Accessibility, built in"
- "On-time delivery, built-in access"
- "Training that turns into jobs"
- "It didn't give me a job. It gave me the skills to go get a job."

## Language to Avoid

- "We make people thrive" — paternalistic
- "Optimized solutions for independence" — jargon
- "Despite vision loss, they thrive" — deficit framing
- "We give jobs to the blind" — objectifying
- "We give back to the community" — performative

## Universal Before/After

### Access and Accommodation

- Before: "We provide accommodations to assist the blind."
- After: "We make access standard for people who are blind or low vision."

### Services

- Before: "Utilize our services for improved independence."
- After: "Use our tools and training to live independently."

### Contact

- Before: "Contact us for assistance."
- After: "How can we help?"

### Research

- Before: "Our research endeavors support innovation."
- After: "Our research turns ideas into tools people can use."

### Employment

- Before: "We give jobs to the blind."
- After: "We create career paths, and paychecks."

### Framing

- Before: "Despite vision loss, they thrive."
- After: "With the right tools, people thrive."

## Before/After by Pillar

### Commercial Solutions

> **Before:** "Envision is a nonprofit organization that provides meaningful employment to people who are blind or visually impaired through our print and fulfillment services."
>
> **After:** "Envision Print Services delivers enterprise-grade print, fulfillment, and assembly solutions. HIPAA compliant, on time, and built to spec. Our workforce is proven. Our capabilities are scalable. Let's talk about what we can do for your operation."

### Vision Rehabilitation Center

> **Before:** "Despite losing most of her vision, Maria didn't give up. Thanks to Envision, she was able to get her life back."
>
> **After:** "Maria didn't know what was possible after her diagnosis. After working with the Envision Vision Rehabilitation Center, through low vision evaluations, assistive technology training, and independent living skills programs, she had the tools to move forward on her own terms."

### Arts Center

> **Before:** "Envision's art program gives blind and visually impaired individuals a creative outlet and therapeutic activity to help them cope with vision loss."
>
> **After:** "The Envision Arts Center is where artists who are blind or low vision make, show, and sell their work. No qualifier needed. Just art, and the community that surrounds it."

### Base Supply Center

> **Before:** "By shopping at our Base Supply Centers, you're giving back to the blind and visually impaired community and supporting our troops."
>
> **After:** "Envision Base Supply Centers operate on installations across the country. Stocked, staffed, and ready. Quality products, reliable supply, and a workforce that understands what service means."

### Child Development Center

> **Before:** "ECDC welcomes children of all abilities in an inclusive environment where every child is accepted."
>
> **After:** "At Envision Child Development Center, kids who are blind or low vision learn alongside their sighted peers from day one. State-of-the-art facilities, future-ready curriculum, and a community built for every child to thrive."

### Mission Services

> **Before:** "Envision provides critical services to the blind and visually impaired community, helping those who suffer from vision loss live better lives."
>
> **After:** "From Heather's Camp to adult day support and beyond, Envision Mission Services is there at every stage. Whatever the need, whatever the moment, no one navigates this alone."

### Research Institute

> **Before:** "Envision is committed to innovative research that helps the visually impaired community."
>
> **After:** "Envision Research Institute partners with universities and industry leaders to turn vision science into tools people actually use. The work is specific, the outcomes are measurable, and the impact is real."

### Workforce Innovation

> **Before:** "Envision helps blind and visually impaired individuals find jobs and become productive members of society."
>
> **After:** "Envision Workforce Innovation builds career-ready skills, assistive technology training, job readiness, and hands-on career exploration, so people who are blind or low vision can compete, get hired, and grow. Training that turns into jobs."

### Continuing Education

> **Before:** "Envision offers continuing education opportunities for professionals in the blindness field."
>
> **After:** "Grand Rounds. The Envision Conference. On-demand courses built for practitioners. Envision brings the field together, so clinicians, educators, and researchers leave with tools they use Monday morning."
