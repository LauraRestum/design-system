## Reading order for new contributors

1. **`our-story.md`** — understand what Envision is and what Thriving means
2. **`brand-rules.md`** — know the non-negotiables before you write anything
3. **`voice-and-tone.md`** — learn how Envision speaks
4. **`pillar-profiles.md`** — find the pillar you're writing for and use its tone
5. **`audience-personas.md`** — understand who you're writing to
6. **`brand-architecture.md`** — reference when working with sub-brand lock-ups or logos
